How To Guide

Fill in your first name, select whether you want a cat or not and which colour cat. Give your cat a name and click adopt. Prepare for the cat distribution system to deliver your cat in 3-5 working days.


Hints and Tips

1. Try using the name Zsolt for a unique cat.

2. If you are struggling to select 'no' rethink your choice.

Thank you for visiting A Cat for Zsolt! We hope you are happy with your hypoallergenic cat.*

*All cats are non-refundable, please check terms & conditions.
